# Contributions

1. A modular Python architecture for Formula 1 race strategy simulation.
2. Configuration-driven deterministic experiments with explicit random seeds.
3. Transparent tyre, fuel, weather and safety-car model components.
4. Pit-stop strategy generation, strategy ranking and Monte Carlo uncertainty analysis.
5. Public-data integration scaffolds that avoid fabricated official or private team data.
6. Dashboard, visualization and documentation scaffolds for engineering education and portfolio use.
