# Future Work

Future work should focus on calibration, validation and richer decision support:

- Calibrate tyre, fuel, weather and pit-loss parameters against public historical data.
- Add richer traffic interaction, blue-flag, penalty, retirement and restart models.
- Implement Bayesian optimisation and genetic algorithms for strategy search.
- Add multi-objective Pareto reporting across race time, risk, tyre life and pit exposure.
- Expand telemetry-driven driver pace estimation with public-data source citations.
- Add exportable engineering reports and reproducible benchmark suites.
